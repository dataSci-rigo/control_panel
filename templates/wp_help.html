Bot Commands

← Dashboard

Setup

/start Begin a new 10-week cycle — choose your challenge type and set your I Want anchor
/reset Archive the current cycle and start fresh (history is preserved)

Daily

/log Log an urge — trigger, intensity (1–5), gave in or resisted, and notes
/today See today's check-in entry, urges logged, and recovery boosters

Weekly

/week This week's theme, Under the Microscope exercise, and experiment
/tips 3–5 practical tips tailored to this week's theme and your challenge AI
/history [N] Read the AI synthesis for Week N (defaults to current week) AI

Info

/status Cycle overview — current week, day, challenge, and start date
/help Show the command list in Telegram
/cancel Exit any in-progress conversation (/start, /log, /reset)
